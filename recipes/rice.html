Ribeye Steak

Cook the perfect ribeye steak using the sous-vide method

Ingredients

  • 1 8 ounce ribeye filet
  • 3 tablespoon salted butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  1. Sprinkle salt and pepper on steak and leave out for at least 30 minutes
  2. Heat sous-vide to a temperature of 134 degrees fahrenheit for medium rare
  3. Put steak in vacuum sealed bag and into sous vide for at least one hour
  4. Sear the steak on grill or smoking hot cast iron
  5. Let rest for at least five minutes before cutting
