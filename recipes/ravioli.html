Ravioli

Low on time? Cook ravioli

Ingredients

  • 1 package ravioli from costco
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  1. Boil water and add salt. The water should taste like the ocean
  2. Put ravioli in water for 3 minutes. Make sure to seperate pieces so they don't stick to each other while cooking
  3. Heat up tomato sauce in a separate saucepan
  4. Drain ravioli from water and mix into sauce
